Who We Are

Committed to Uganda's Natural Heritage

Greening International Organization (GIO) Uganda is a premier environmental NGO dedicated to restoring ecosystems, fighting climate change, and promoting sustainable agriculture including livestock farming.

Our Mission

To empower Ugandan communities through active participation in environmental conservation and sustainable resource management.

Our Vision

A greener, resilient Uganda where humanity and nature coexist in perfect harmony for generations to come.

What We Do

Our Core Initiatives

We tackle environmental challenges through strategic, community-driven projects designed to create lasting impact.

Afforestation

Massive tree-planting campaigns across deforested regions to restore green cover and air quality.

Animal Farming

Promoting sustainable livestock and animal farming practices that support community livelihoods while respecting the land.

Eco-Education

Integrating environmental awareness into school curricula and hosting community workshops.

Waste Management

Promoting recycling and proper waste disposal techniques to keep Ugandan cities clean.
